About Me

I'm a physics graduate with a Master's degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and a Bachelor's from City University of Hong Kong. My journey in physics has led me to specialize in computational methods and cold atom physics, where I combine theoretical understanding with practical programming skills.


Currently expanding my expertise in cold atom physics, particularly studying P-wave Feshbach molecules and ultracold quantum systems. My work bridges the gap between complex quantum phenomena and computational solutions, making abstract physics concepts tangible through simulation and analysis.
